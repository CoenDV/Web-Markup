Online coaching

Don't want to rely on specific gym hours or are you to shy to go to the gym? Maybe online coaching is the thing for you, the services can be live or a video so a 24/7 service is guaranteed, the rates for a lesson are also lower than its physical counterpart.

Alone time

Most gyms aren't opened 24/7 so with online coaching you can make your own scheme to train. Another benefit is that you are most likely to do this alone with a coach so there are no bystanders that can look at or judge you.

What you need

All that is needed for the service is to have a portable device with stable internet connection to either watch and follow videos or host a livestream (with an app such as teams or zoom). The portable device adviced would be a laptop or tablet with a big screen. mobile phones often have too small screens to see all details of an excersice.

Populairity

Online coaching is also popular with celebrities such as actors. For a lot of roles actors need to get in shape, while it was normal for them to fly over and train with coaches now they can stay at home and train from the confort of home. The same profesionnal coaches teaching the actors but now they can stay at home longer with their family before leaving to shoot the actual movie.

What to expect

When using our services you can expect profesionnal coaches to help you train using a livestream, either alone or in small groups. All out coaches are certified to give advice and will always happily answer all your questions. Livestreams isn't the only service we are providing, we also share videos of complete training schemes and diet plans.

Machine Learning

Using machine learning and feedback from our coaches we are constantly improving out services and schemes. Technologies such as wearable fitness trackers can be implemented in the near future to give more feedback to both client and coach.
